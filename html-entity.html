&#nnnn; or &#xhhhh; or &name;

  • nnnn is the code point in decimal form
  • hhhh is the code point in hexadecimal form
  • name is the case-sensitive name of the entity
  • x must be lowercase in XML documents
  • nnnn or hhhh may be any number of digits and may include leading zeros
  • hhhh may mix uppercase and lowercase, though uppercase is the usual style
